wedding festivities

July 1, 2017 starting at 5:30p
Dress code: Black tie optional

Our wedding day will take place in different sections of the Chicago Botanic Garden
1000 Lake Cook Rd, Glencoe, IL 60022

Ceremony at 5:30p

Krasberg Rose Garden

Weather permitting, the ceremony will be held outdoors at the Krasberg Rose Garden. Please plan to arrive 15 minutes early.

Cocktails at 6p

English Walled Garden

Cocktail hour will immediately follow the ceremony. Enjoy a cocktail (or two!) and some delicious hor d'oeuvres.

Reception at 7p

McGinley Pavilion

Join us for food and festivities under the stars. Bring your dancing shoes and your best moves - get ready to celebrate!

How to get there:

If you choose to stay in downtown Chicago, you can take the Metra train from Chicago Olgilvie Transportation Center to the wedding location. Trains come every 1-2 hours and our venue is only about a mile away from the Glencoe stop on the Union Pacific North Line.

If you'd like to drive, pull up to the gate and tell them that you're with David and Jeanette's wedding. Parking is free for wedding guests. There isn't a specific lot sectioned off for guests, but you should be able to find a parking spot in either Lot 1 or Lot 2.

Once you arrive at the venue, walk straight through the Visitor Center and follow the signs to the ceremony.
